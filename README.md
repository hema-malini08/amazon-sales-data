# Amazon product Sales Analysis

## Problem Statement
In a competitive e-commerce marketplace, understanding what drives product demand is critical.  
This analysis evaluates how paid promotions (sponsored listings) and product category performance influence purchase behavior, in order to identify key growth drivers and optimize marketing and inventory strategies.

## Business Questions
- Which product categories dominate demand?  
- Do sponsored products generate more purchases than organic products?

## Tools Used
- **Python** – Data analysis and cleaning workflow  
- **Pandas** – Data manipulation, cleaning, and aggregation  
- **Google Colab** – Cloud-based environment for running and sharing notebooks  
  
## Key Insights
- **Sponsored vs Organic Products**  
  Sponsored products show significantly higher average purchases compared to organic products, indicating that paid promotions improve product visibility and drive conversions.

- **Category Performance**  
  *Power & Batteries* is the leading category, accounting for **26.17M purchases** and contributing approximately **60–65% of total demand** in the dataset.  
  This category significantly outperforms all others.

## Business Recommendations
- Increase investment in sponsored ads for high-performing products  
- Focus marketing and inventory strategies on the *Power & Batteries* category  
- Ensure stock availability for top-selling products to avoid lost sales  
- Use targeted ads to boost visibility of low-performing categories
